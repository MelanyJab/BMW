About Us

BMW was created in 1917 from the munich firm Rapp-Motorenwerke. The company was incorporated into Knorr-Bremse AG in 1920 before being refounded as BMW AG in 1922. It was the successor of Bayerische Flugzeugwerke AG, founded in 1916. Therefore 1916 is considered the founding year. BMW entered the automobile business in 1928. Our company's R32 motorcycle set a world speed record that was not broken until 1937. Now BMW is known for its focus on delivering exceptional driving experiences. Our vehicles are engineered to provide precise handling, responsive acceleration and a sporty driving feel.
